    About

    Bhuwaneshwari Middle School with its rich and glorious history stands out in the crowd.

    History

    It is one of the oldest schools of Ara city. The establishment year of school is unknown but most people predict it was established in around 1887 AD. The school has been named after one of its renowed student, Mr. Bhuwaneshwar Prasad Sinha (6th Chief Justice of Supreme Court of India).

    Earlier known as Mahajani School.

    Renowed students of our school:-

    • Mr. Jagjiwan Ram(Former deputy prime-minister of India)
    • Mr. Ram Shubhag Singh(Former central Railway Minister of India)
    • Mr. Ambika Sharan Singh(Former Finance Minister of Government of Bihar)
